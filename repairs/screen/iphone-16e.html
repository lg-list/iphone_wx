Before you begin

Insert an opening pick

The screen consists of a display panel and a bezel. Make sure you insert the pick under the bezel, or you risk separating the two layers and destroying the screen. Apply a suction handle to the bottom edge of the screen. While holding the frame down with one hand, pull up on the handle with a strong, steady force to create a gap between the screen and the frame. Insert the tip of an opening pick into the gap.
