01

Prepare your iPhone for disassembly

Let your phone's battery drain below 25% before starting this repair—a charged lithium‑ion battery may catch fire if damaged. Unplug all cables from your phone. Hold the power and either volume button and slide to power off your phone.

Insert an opening pick

Screen information

As you slice the adhesive securing the screen in the next steps, be careful of the following areas: There are two delicate cables connecting the screen to the phone: one just above the action button, and the other near the middle of the left edge. There are multiple spring contacts around the perimeter of the phone. Be extra careful not to insert your pick deeper than suggested in these locations to avoid bending the contacts.
