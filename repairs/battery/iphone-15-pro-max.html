01

Prepare your phone for disassembly

Allow your phone's battery to drain below 25%, as a charged lithium-ion battery is a potential safety hazard. Unplug any cables from your phone. Hold the power and either volume buttons and slide to power off your phone.

Screen information

As you slice the adhesive securing the screen in the next steps, be careful of the following areas: There are two delicate cables connecting the screen to the phone: one just above the action button, and the other near the middle of the left edge.
